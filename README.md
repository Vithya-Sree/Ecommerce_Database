📊 Retail Sales Data Analysis Using Excel 2007

Executive Summary
This project analyzes over **2,000 retail sales transactions** using **Microsoft Excel 2007**, integrating Customer, Product, Store, and Sales data into a single master dataset through **VLOOKUP-based relational modeling**. The objective was to uncover sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, regional performance, and product opportunities to support business decision-making.

🎯 Business Objective

The analysis aimed to answer key business questions:
* Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
* Which regions and store formats perform best?
* How does customer loyalty impact spending?
* What are the yearly sales trends?
* Which payment methods are preferred by customers?

---

📈 Key Findings

1. Regional Performance

* North Region - generated the highest revenue (₹643,579).
* West Region - significantly underperformed compared to other regions.

2. Product Category Performance

* Sports (₹542,010) and Electronics (₹504,863) were the top revenue-generating categories.
* Together, they contributed nearly half of total sales revenue.

3. Customer Loyalty Analysis

* Platinum customers generated the highest revenue (₹672,587).
* Bronze customers also contributed strongly, highlighting the importance of both customer acquisition and retention.

4. Store Type Performance

* Online Stores achieved the highest revenue (₹914,148).
* Flagship stores performed well, while Outlet stores lagged behind.

5. Sales Trend Analysis

* Revenue increased significantly from 2023 to 2024.
* Sales declined during 2025, indicating a potential slowdown in demand or increased market competition.

6. Payment Method Insights

* Credit Card and PayPal were the most commonly used payment methods.
* Cash on Delivery (COD) had lower adoption.

✅ Conclusion

This project demonstrates how meaningful business insights can be generated using Excel 2007 despite the absence of modern features such as Power Pivot, Data Models, Slicers, and Timelines. By combining relational datasets through VLOOKUP and leveraging Pivot Tables, formulas, and visual dashboards, the analysis provides actionable recommendations for category growth, regional expansion, customer retention, and sales optimization.
